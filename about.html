About DabbeWale

DabbeWale is a platform designed to connect users with local tiffin and home-cooked meal providers. Our mission is to make nutritious, affordable, and delicious home-style food accessible to everyone, while supporting small food businesses and home chefs.

Project Features

User authentication and profile management
Provider discovery and menu browsing
Order management and real-time tracking
Rating and review system
Location-based provider search
Admin dashboard and analytics
Modern, responsive web design

Project Status

The frontend is live and fully functional for core UI/UX. Backend API development and integration are ongoing. Advanced features and business logic are planned for future phases.

Our Vision

We aim to empower local food providers and offer users a healthy alternative to fast food, all through a seamless digital experience.
