About DabbeWale

DabbeWale is your trusted platform for discovering and ordering fresh, home-cooked tiffin meals from local kitchens. We connect busy professionals, students, and families with passionate home chefs and small tiffin providers, making healthy, affordable, and delicious food accessible every day.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the warmth and nutrition of home-cooked meals to your doorstep, while empowering local cooks and small food businesses to thrive. We believe everyone deserves access to wholesome, hygienic, and tasty food—just like you’d get at home.

How It Works

Browse Local Tiffins: Discover a variety of home-cooked meal options from trusted providers in your area.
Order Easily: Select your favorite tiffin, customize your meal, and place an order in just a few clicks.
Fresh & Hygienic: All meals are prepared with care, using quality ingredients and maintaining high hygiene standards.
Timely Delivery: Enjoy prompt delivery to your home, office, or campus—hot and ready to eat.
Support Local Chefs: Every order helps independent cooks and small businesses grow.

Why Choose DabbeWale?

  • Authentic Taste: Experience the flavors of real home kitchens, not commercial kitchens.
  • Affordable Meals: Enjoy nutritious food at pocket-friendly prices.
  • Flexible Plans: Order one-time or subscribe for daily/weekly tiffins as per your needs.
  • Community Driven: We foster a community of food lovers and passionate home chefs.

Health & Safety

We prioritize your health and safety. All our partner kitchens follow strict hygiene protocols, use fresh ingredients, and maintain cleanliness at every step. Our delivery partners are trained to handle food with care, ensuring your meal reaches you safely and hygienically.

Sustainability

DabbeWale encourages eco-friendly practices by promoting reusable containers and minimizing food waste. We work with providers who care about the environment and strive to make every meal a step towards a greener future.

Community Impact

By choosing DabbeWale, you support local entrepreneurs, empower home chefs (especially women), and help build a stronger, healthier community. Every meal you order is a vote for local talent and small business growth.
